UX Principles

"Anxiety is our biggest competitor."

Progressive Disclosure

Surface primary info first; hide complex disclosures to prevent cognitive overload.

One-Click Flows

Minimize steps to reduce friction and lower Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC).

Visual Progress Tracking

Show users where they are in the journey to build confidence and reduce drop-off.
